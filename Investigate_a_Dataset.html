Conclusions¶

The report contain 5 questions to be answered :

1st question Is There a correlation between the budget and revenue? We found a slight linear relation in scatter diagram however in bar chart no relation.

2nd question What is the most movie has gained the highest votes? the answer was somehow surprised as the movie 'The story of film: An Odyssey' by some searching that movie was an old and long runtime.

3rd question What is the top 10 movies gain revenue? as expected the 1st 3D movie was the highest revenue gained along till 2015.

4th question Is the movie got high count vote got high vote average? the answer was yes at certain average vote the vote count start increasing.

5th question Which genres generating the highest revenues? Action genre is the highest revenue among the genres.
